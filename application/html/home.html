Blaze is an online tutoring system, for conducting online classes, discussions, online exams using a single platform. We provide you user-friendly portals for teachers as well as students and automated exam proctoring and plagiarism check. Our platform provides various portals for academic activities like assignments, study materials, video lectures, live classes etc. Blaze has also integrated an online examination portal, which provides AI proctoring during exams. Still got doubts about network issues and security? Don’t worry ! All data is processed at the user side and we have taken immense care to protect your privacy.
